ABOUT US

What are we?

We are a non-profit, non-government organization that allows one to donate as well as seek help from us. We collect funds online as well as offline and provide the same to the various causes we suport. Also, the people who seek help from us are reached out via our executives so we can help them in each way possible.

How do we function?

We mainly work in Four sectors at the moment that are Natural calamities, Education, NGOs and Medical aids. Donations are made on the basis of these four sectors and then the amounts are funded to the people in need depending upon these sectors. People who are in need of donations or other help can also contact us and we consider them too for further donations and help. Non-capital donations can also be made by dropping by the donation stuff at our given address.

Want to donate something else?

If you wish to donate food items, clothing, daily essentials or anything other than money, you are more than welcome here. Please feel free to drop your donations at our address given below. We'd be highly grateful if you could help in any way.
